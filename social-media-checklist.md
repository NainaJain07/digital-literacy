# 📱 Social Media Responsibility Checklist

## 📌 Overview
This document provides a simple checklist of Do's and Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student. It aims to promote safe, respectful, and mindful online behavior.

---

## ✅ Do's
- Think before posting; ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
- Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal information.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
- Engage positively and respectfully in online discussions.

---

## ❌ Don'ts
- Don’t share sensitive personal information (address, phone number, passwords).
- Don’t post offensive, abusive, or harmful content.
- Don’t accept friend requests from unknown or suspicious accounts.
- Don’t click on unverified or suspicious links.
- Don’t spend excessive time on social media affecting studies or health.

---

## 🎯 Purpose
The purpose of this checklist is to:
- Encourage responsible digital behavior  
- Promote online safety and privacy  
- Reduce risks like cyberbullying, phishing, and data misuse  


---

## ✅ Conclusion
Following these simple guidelines can help students maintain a positive digital presence and stay safe online while using social media platforms.
